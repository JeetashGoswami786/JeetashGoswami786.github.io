Three Phase Synchronous Generator

2025

A synchronous generator converts mechanical energy into electrical energy by rotating a magnetic field at a constant speed synchronized with the grid frequency. It is widely used in power plants due to its ability to provide stable voltage and frequency control. Its excitation system allows precise regulation of reactive power and system stability.
